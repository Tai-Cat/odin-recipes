Vegan Spring Rolls

Ingredients

  • Rice Paper Wraps
  • Cucumber
  • Carrots
  • Rice Noodles
  • Mushrooms
  • Tofo
  • Avocado

Ingredients for the peanut sauce

  • Hot water
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables and tofo thinly
  2. Cook the noodles and place to the side
  3. Boil some hot water and place some aside in a bowl
  4. Mix the peanut butter, hoisin sauce and hot water to make the peanut sauce
  5. Dip the rice paper wraps in the hot water then lay down on a flat surface
  6. Place the vegetable, tofo and rice noodles in the wrap
  7. Wrap everything up and then place aside with the peanut sauce
