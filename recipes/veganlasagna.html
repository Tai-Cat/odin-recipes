Vegan Lasagna

This is a vegan lasagna that my family has been making at home for many years.

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms
  • Aubergine
  • Zucchini
  • Bell Pepper
  • Onion
  • Cashews
  • Water
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Make the cashew sauce with the cashews, water, lemon juice and salt
  2. Cook Onions and tomatos and then blend them to make the tomato sauce
  3. Roast the vegetables in the oven until they're mostly cooked
